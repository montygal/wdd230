News Release!

We've got some exciting things happening this winter! Join us for: tobogganing, snow ball throwing contests, building snowmen and snow angels and ice skating on the lake. Warm up afterwards with a cup of hot chocolate by our cozy fireplace and mingle in our commons with the other guests. Enjoy catching a trip on one of our boats to tour and see the whales. You might even see a puffin or two! The puffin is Newfoundland's bird. Don't forget that this is the prime time of the year for storm watching. The waves are beautiful, but keep your distance so you don't get swept away. The only sweeping away you'll get here is off your feet at the beauty our island has to offer.
