I've picked three colors to try and get a feel for what I want the website to look like. The first is CornflowerBlue #6495ED. I want to include blue as one of the main colors for an ocean feel.

The second color is Azure #FOFFFF. It is an off-white looking color that I think will blend nicely with the blue. I don't want too many bold colors, because then my website will be overwhelming.

The third color I picked was Brown #A52A2A and I think it complements the other two colors nicely. I want to use it for background borders and other important information.
