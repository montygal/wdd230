Site name: Explore Spaniard's Bay!

The site purpose is to draw tourists to Spaniard's Bay, Newfoundland. Newfoundland is the Eastern most province of Canada. Spaniard's Bay is a tiny fishing village in the heart of Newfoundland. I want to list fun activities, local food and sight-seeing opportunities.

The domain name is going to be nfld.info. It was a suggested name for the site when I looked at available domain names.
