There's a lot to see in Los Angeles!

Second in size only to New York, Los Angeles

is a bustling city with lots to see and do.

You'll find cities like Beverly Hills, Long Beach

Santa Catalina and more! You'll find the most

population in downtown Los Angeles, where there are up

to 50,000 people living per sqaure mile.

Los Angeles is famous for Hollywood!

Famous for Hollywood, and famous for fruit! With

so many people living here, our smoothie business is

booming. You'll find the Hollywood walk of fame, and visitors

will often check to see if their favorite actor or actress

is there, along with taking a picture to preserve the memory.

Highlight of San Francisco. The bridge!

One of the highlights of the city is the San Francisco

bridge, but we've got lots of additional fun facts about

the area! Did you know, the city has it's own fog? Also,

the Chinese fortune cookie was born there. Sea lions weren't

always around, making their first appearance in 1989. That's not

too long ago! And our last fun fact: the city has over 50 hills!

Look at that view!

While you're in San Francisco, you'll want to visit the ocean.

There is a beach called Ocean Beach located in the Golden Gate

National Parks Conservancy. It's on the west coast and offers some

of the most breathtaking views around. Everyone loves the ocean,

and this beach won't disappoint! Don't forget, if you visit in the

winter months, you'll enjoy the pasttime of storm watching. Usually

tourists want to go during the summer, but true ocean viewers will take

advantage of the storm watching activities that only appear during the winter months.
