Fresh Fruit For All!

Our journey started 50 years ago when John Phillips decided he wanted to make eating healthy a personal journey and discovered his love of fresh fruits and veggies. He wanted to share what he had learned with anyone who had a desire to live a healthier lifestyle, and that is how Bountiful Foods started. We start by finding the freshest fruits and vegetables from local farmers here in California, and then let you customize your favorites for unique smoothies that have gained popularity with over 1000 individuals right here in Calsvad and in the surrounding area. What started as one man's journey has led to a successful and healthy way to boost your energy in the morning. We package and freeze your special request, so that all you need to do is put it in the blender for a quick and easy breakfast. Take a look at our options and customize your smoothies today!
